What is Nature?

Natures are characteristics that subtly influence how a Pokémon's stats grow as it gains experience.

Let's check pokemon Mr.Mime as an example:

Each Pokémon can have a Nature that affects two of its basic stats, boosting one by 10% while decreasing another by 10%. There are 25 different Natures, with various combinations of boosts and decreases.
